## Policies

### Shipping
Free white-glove delivery on furniture orders over $1,500 (continental US).
Standard parcel shipping free on orders over $150. Furniture delivered with
inside-room placement and packaging removal. International freight quoted
case-by-case.

### Returns
60-day returns on stock items (buyer pays return freight on furniture).
Made-to-order items final sale except for defects. Linens and textiles
returnable unused with original packaging.

### Warranty & Repair
- Lifetime structural warranty on solid-wood furniture
- 5-year warranty on hardware and joinery
- 2-year warranty on textiles against manufacturing defects
- Repair service available — ship piece back, we repair at cost, return
  shipping included for items under warranty

### Payment Methods
